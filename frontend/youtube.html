How it works

All processing runs locally using open-source tools — no third-party services involved:

  • yt-dlp — extracts video metadata, locates the best audio-only stream (AAC or Opus), and downloads it with real-time speed reporting
  • FFmpeg libmp3lame — transcodes raw stream or local file to MP3 at your chosen bitrate with embedded ID3 title tags
  • FFmpeg anlmdn — AI non-local means denoising to reduce background hiss and noise (optional)
  • FFmpeg loudnorm — EBU R128 broadcast loudness normalization for consistent playback volume (optional)

⚠ Playlists are capped at 50 videos per batch. Files are not stored in the container — they are delivered directly to your browser or saved to the host library volume.
